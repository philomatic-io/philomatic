# Philosophy (Kindly Request that Only Humans Edit :pray:)

The world is increasingly becoming reliant on AI for information and for critical thought.

* Let's create tools that foster growth in media and research literacy and empower people to keep learning in the AI age.
* Let's embrace tools to make learning the analog way even better.
* Let's democratize modeling how we achieve our understanding of the world so we can promote ways of learning that are most helpful.
* Let's work together to provide structure to the way information is ingested and distributed.
